Regression Testing

Core Service

Regression Testing

Every new feature or bug fix introduces the risk of breaking existing functionality. Systematic regression testing ensures your product remains stable through every release cycle, giving your team confidence to ship frequently.

I design and execute regression suites tailored to your application's critical paths, so you can move fast without breaking things.

  • Full and partial regression suite execution
  • Release-readiness assessment
  • Regression coverage mapping
  • Pre- and post-deployment validation
  • Documented pass/fail results with metrics
  • Defect lifecycle tracking and reporting
