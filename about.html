My Background

Results-Driven. Quality-Focused.

With over 20 years of experience in software testing across a wide range of applications built on technologies such as .NET, ColdFusion, Java, and more, I bring a deep understanding of the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) and strong problem-solving skills to every project.

My background also includes project management, with a focus on developing and executing comprehensive roadmaps and timelines to ensure successful delivery. I have a proven track record of identifying and mitigating potential risks before they become costly problems.

"Dedicated to ensuring every application meets the highest standards of quality and performance."

I have an in-depth quality assurance background across a diverse range of software industries, including healthcare, education, biotechnology, transportation, e-Commerce, and gaming. As a results-driven, quality-focused professional, I emphasize clarity, strategic efficiency, and streamlined processes to consistently deliver high-impact, reliable outcomes.

I am committed to building high-performing QA processes, optimizing testing workflows, and ensuring software meets both user expectations and industry standards.
